Wifi Car

I have successfully developed a WiFi car that can be controlled using any mobile device through the Blink app. This innovative project utilizes the Blink app as an intermediary between the internet and the car, allowing for seamless communication. By employing a NodeMCU, the car's wheels can be controlled by receiving signals transmitted from the Blink app. This connectivity is established through an API, enabling real-time control and operation of the car from a mobile device. The integration of the Blink app, NodeMCU, and API has resulted in a versatile and efficient WiFi car that offers an immersive and user-friendly experience.
